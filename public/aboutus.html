Our Story

Welcome to The Green Bowl, where passion for delicious food meets innovation in delivery. Founded in 2023 , we set out with a mission to make high-quality, diverse food options easily accessible to everyone in our community.

Meet Our Founder

SURYA RAMIAH - Founder & CEO

Surya embarked on this journey with a vision to revolutionize the food delivery experience. He brings 2 years of experience in this field and a deep love for food.. Driven by a commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, Surya has overseen every aspect of The Green Bowl's growth.His dedication to quality and innovation is reflected in our diverse menu and our seamless delivery service.

Our Mission

At The Green Bowl, we believe that good food brings people together. Our mission is to provide you with an exceptional dining experience from the comfort of your home. We are dedicated to sourcing fresh ingredients, supporting local chefs, and ensuring prompt, friendly service.

Our Values

Quality

We only partner with trusted vendors and chefs to ensure top-notch food.

Customer Satisfaction

Your happiness is our priority.we’re here to make sure you have a delightful experience with every bite.

Innovation

We continuously seek new ways to enhance your food delivery experience.
